A Tribute to Dr. A.P.J. Abdul Kalam

The Missile Man of India

About

Dr. A.P.J. Abdul Kalam was an Indian aerospace scientist and the 11th President of India. He is admired worldwide for his humility, intelligence, and contribution to science and education. Known as the *People’s President*, Dr. Kalam inspired millions of students to believe in the power of dreams.

His Contributions

  • Played a major role in India’s missile and nuclear programs
  • Developed India's first Satellite Launch Vehicle (SLV)
  • Author of inspiring books like *Wings of Fire* and *Ignited Minds*
  • Promoted education and youth empowerment

Famous Quote

“Dream, dream, dream. Dreams transform into thoughts and thoughts result in action.”
